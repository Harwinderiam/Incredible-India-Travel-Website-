Travel Tips

Essential tips and guides for a smooth travel experience in India.

Essential Travel Tips

Visa and Documentation

Ensure you have a valid visa and keep copies of your passport and travel documents.

Currency and Payments

Carry local currency (INR) and use digital payment apps like UPI for convenience.

Food and Water Safety

Drink bottled water and eat at reputable restaurants to avoid food-related issues.

Packing Essentials

Pack light, comfortable clothing, and don't forget adapters for Indian power sockets.
